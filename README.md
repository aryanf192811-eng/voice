Citizen Dashboard – Overview & Activity Monitoring

This screen presents the citizen home dashboard of the platform, offering a real-time snapshot of personal trust metrics, case progress, and civic activity. Users can quickly view reliability score, open vs. resolved complaints, weekly submission patterns, and category-wise resolution distribution. The interface also surfaces geo-tagged nearby risk alerts and recent verification actions, enabling faster awareness and informed participation within the local governance network.

Incident Reporting & Secure Submission

This interface enables structured, verifiable issue reporting with category tagging, location input, priority calibration, and descriptive context. Citizens can attach optional media evidence and provide contact details for investigator follow-ups. Built-in safeguards handle spam filtering, validation, and automatic routing to the responsible authority. A running log of recent tickets ensures transparency and continuity after submission.

Public Incident Intelligence Feed

A prioritized stream of verified civic reports ranked by safety impact and reliability signals. Users can filter by validation status or severity, search by location or case ID, and quickly assess ground reality through visual evidence. Each entry exposes confidence scoring and routing state, enabling transparent situational awareness before deeper investigation.

Citizen Reputation & Contribution Profile

This screen reflects an individual’s civic credibility through a composite score derived from accuracy, verification history, and case outcomes. Quantitative metrics such as reports filed, resolutions influenced, and validation rate build measurable trust. Achievement badges reward constructive participation and consistent reliability. The model incentivizes high-quality inputs while strengthening accountability across the network.
